Top 5 things to do in Kyoto, Japan
  1. Visit Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion): Explore the stunning Zen Buddhist temple covered in gold leaf and set amidst beautiful gardens. It's one of Kyoto's most iconic landmarks.
  2. Experience a Tea Ceremony: Kyoto is renowned for its traditional tea ceremonies. Participate in one to savor matcha green tea and gain insights into Japanese culture.
  3. Explore Fushimi Inari Taisha: Walk through thousands of vibrant orange torii gates at this Shinto shrine, dedicated to the god of rice and agriculture. The hike to the summit offers breathtaking views.
  4. Wander Through Gion District: Stroll the historic streets of Gion, known for its preserved traditional wooden machiya houses and geisha culture. You might even spot a geisha or maiko (apprentice geisha) in the area.
  5. Discover Arashiyama: Head to the Arashiyama district to visit the enchanting bamboo grove, take a scenic boat ride down the Hozugawa River, and explore the beautiful Tenryu-ji Temple and its gardens.
